Arrays are ordered lists that hold multiple items together. Think of them like a row of labeled boxes, a shopping list, or a train with connected cars â€” each has a position, and you can refer to any one by its place in line.

ğŸ’¡ How Arrays Work
Every item in an array has an index â€” its position number. To keep counting consistent, programming starts at 0, not 1.

So for let colors = ['red', 'green', 'blue']:

    colors[0] is 'red' (first item)
    colors[1] is 'green' (second item)
